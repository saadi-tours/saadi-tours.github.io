Saadi Tours

A tour guide based in Uganda

Hello everyone, my name is Sheldra Saadi Miilo of Uganda. I am a professional tour guide and a member of Uganda Safari Guides Association (USAGA). I have been in the tourism industry for over seven years.

Where I work

I do safaris in Uganda and across East Africa. I can do safaris in all parks of Uganda:

  • Murchison Falls National Park
  • Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Kidepo Valley National Park
  • Lake Mburo National Park
  • Gorilla tracking in Bwindi and Mgahinga
  • And many more - just ask me

Outside of Uganda, I do safaris in Kenya:

  • Masai Mara National Park
  • And more

Tanzania:

  • Serengeti National Park

Rwanda:

  • Akagera National Park
  • Nyungwe National Park

I can also organize mountain hiking in the Rwenzoris, Mt. Elgon, and Nyiragongo in the DRC.

City and Culture Tours

I also organize city tours all over Uganda and Rwanda.

Rates

As for now, I conduct budget safaris for students, volunteers, and anyone who wants to see Uganda affordably. For most trips, it is best to contact me for information about pricing.


Murchison Falls

Many of my clients are interested in visiting Murchison. My 3-day Murchison package includes accommodation with full board, rhino trekking, one game drive, and a boat ride to the falls. It does not include the first lunch on the way to the park.

  • For one or two people, $600 per person
  • For three or more people, $400 per person

Lake Mburo

My two-day budget safari to Lake Mburo includes full board accommodation in permanent safari tents, a night game drive, a boat ride on Lake Mburo, a morning game drive, and transport there and back.

  • For one or two people, $520 per person
  • For three or more people, $220 per person
